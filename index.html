Why symmetric?

Balance is engineered in, not added on

The "symmetric" in our name reflects a fundamental design philosophy — every circuit, every channel, every algorithm is built with perfect bilateral symmetry. This eliminates measurement bias, reduces thermal hotspots, and maximizes the useful life of every cell in your pack.
