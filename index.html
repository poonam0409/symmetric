What We Do

Core Capabilities

End-to-end electronic engineering — from schematic to production-ready hardware, with precision at every step.

Firmware Development

End-to-end embedded firmware development for ARM, RISC-V, and microcontroller platforms — from bare-metal drivers to RTOS-based applications tailored to your hardware.

Hardware Development

Complete hardware design services from schematic capture to prototype validation — covering analog, digital, power, and mixed-signal systems for industrial and commercial applications.

Software Development

Custom website and mobile app development — from responsive business websites and e-commerce platforms to cross-platform mobile apps, built with modern frameworks and a focus on performance and user experience.

PCB Design & Layout

High-density, multi-layer PCB layout with impedance control, EMI management, signal integrity analysis, and full DFM compliance for reliable, production-ready boards.

Reverse Engineering & Cost Reduction

Expert analysis of existing hardware to recreate, modernise, or optimise designs — identifying component substitutions and design efficiencies to significantly reduce BOM costs.

Maintenance

Comprehensive support and maintenance services for deployed electronic systems — including firmware updates, hardware diagnostics, component lifecycle management, and long-term reliability assurance.
