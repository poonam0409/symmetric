# About Us

## Who We Are

SymmetricCircuits is a precision engineering company specializing in intelligent Battery Management Systems (BMS) for electric vehicles, grid-scale energy storage, and industrial applications. We design, develop, and manufacture BMS solutions that are balanced, reliable, and built to scale — from compact e-bike packs to heavy-industry deployments.

Our mission is simple: to deliver power intelligence that is as precise as it is dependable, enabling the next generation of electrified systems to perform at their full potential.

---

## Our Name, Our Philosophy

The word *"symmetric"* is not just a name — it is the foundation of everything we build.

Every circuit, every channel, and every algorithm at SymmetricCircuits is designed with perfect bilateral symmetry. This core philosophy eliminates measurement bias, reduces thermal hotspots, and maximises the useful life of every cell in your battery pack. Symmetry is not an aesthetic choice; it is an engineering discipline.

---

## What We Do

We engineer precision BMS modules that provide:

- **Real-time cell monitoring** — Sub-millisecond voltage, current, and temperature sensing across every cell in the stack.
- **Multi-layer protection** — Overvoltage, undervoltage, overcurrent, and thermal runaway prevention enforced at both hardware and firmware level.
- **Patented symmetric active balancing** — Uniform cell voltages achieved with minimal energy loss, extending pack life and maximising efficiency.
- **Universal communication support** — Full multi-protocol compatibility for seamless integration with any powertrain, inverter, or SCADA system.
